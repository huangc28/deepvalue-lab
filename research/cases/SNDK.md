## What the Price Implied

At US$931.16, the market is now underwriting more than a reasonable bull case. Even at a 13x bull-style multiple, the stock implies about US$71.6 of FY2027 EPS, above my bull-case EPS of US$69.8. The key mismatch is still that datacenter was only about 15% of Q2 FY2026 revenue, so the stock is capitalizing durability and mix shift that the current revenue base has not yet proven.

## Key Modeling Decisions

- Used FY2027 normalized non-GAAP EPS x target P/E as the primary lens because Q3 FY2026 guidance looked too strong to capitalize as a permanent run-rate.
- Kept EV/sales as the main cross-check; it remains useful mainly to show how much sentiment has already outrun the current revenue base, while FCF yield is now too distorted by committed cash uses to be the main tension check.
- Set bear / base / bull fair values at US$370.80 / US$631.20 / US$907.40 using US$37.10 / US$52.60 / US$69.80 of FY2027 non-GAAP EPS and 10x / 12x / 13x target multiples.
- Treated the Kioxia and Nanya commitments as mixed items: good for supply continuity, but negative for the quality of free-cash-flow-based rerating arguments. Treated the February secondary offering as a multiple-support item only, not an earnings-power change.

## Risk Lessons

- For pure-play NAND names, do not confuse "AI storage narrative" with proven revenue mix. If datacenter is still a small share of sales, the stock can outrun the business quickly.
- A low-looking forward P/E can be misleading when analyst consensus already embeds an extreme two-year revenue and EPS step-up.
- Once a cyclical memory name trades above bull fair value without a new operating disclosure, the analytical burden shifts from "is the story real?" to "how much mean reversion is the market refusing to price?"
- JV extensions and strategic component deals can improve operating resilience while still capping valuation because they absorb cash and add execution or capital-allocation risk.
- When the stock trades near bull fair value while the technical setup is extended, the right conclusion can still be "thesis intact, valuation rich."
- When the stock trades above bull fair value and the chart is still stretched, "thesis intact" should not soften the valuation call.
