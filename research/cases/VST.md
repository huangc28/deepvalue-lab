## What the Price Implied

At US$163.46, the market is no longer valuing Vistra like a plain merchant generator. The shares are pricing the 2026 hedge-supported floor, most of the stand-alone 2027 opportunity, a largely clean Cogentrix close, and meaningful durability from the Meta and AWS nuclear agreements. The stock is still capitalizing a large part of the all-in 2027 EBITDA uplift before every piece of that per-share value is fully proven.

## Key Modeling Decisions

- Used 2027 all-in EV / Ongoing Operations Adjusted EBITDA as the primary lens because hedging marks, depreciation, acquisition financing, and tax items make P/E much less decision-useful for this business.
- Used equity FCFbG yield as the cross-check because Vistra is explicitly managed for buybacks, dividends, and leverage targets.
- Modeled post-Cogentrix share count at roughly 343.55M to capture the planned 5M-share issuance on top of the current share base, instead of pretending portfolio growth drops into current shares for free.
- Modeled bear / base / bull fair values at US$115.56 / US$155.73 / US$197.93 using US$7.4B / US$8.0B / US$8.6B of 2027 all-in EBITDA, 8.0x / 9.0x / 10.0x EV / EBITDA, and about US$19.5B / US$18.5B / US$18.0B of net debt.
- Treated the Meta and AWS agreements mainly as duration and quality upgrades, not as immediate full-value 2026 earnings lifts.
- Treated the April 2026 unsecured notes pricing as lower financing friction and a cleaner debt ladder, not as incremental operating value by itself.

## Risk Lessons

- Treat Vistra as a hedged merchant-power and retail hybrid, not as a regulated utility and not as a simple P/E stock.
- Long-duration nuclear contracts and investment-grade ratings genuinely improve quality, but the stock can still become full if the market capitalizes those upgrades before per-share proof is visible.
- Acquisition math must be modeled on an all-in per-share basis; higher EBITDA alone is not enough if debt and stock issuance rise at the same time.
- Refinancing headlines can support the valuation ceiling, but they should not be counted as new value unless net debt or post-close share count actually improves.
- For this business type, current-price-implies work matters a lot once the market starts re-rating the company from commodity-sensitive generator to scarcity-power platform.
- The hidden support is hedge coverage plus retail integration. The main hidden risk is paying simultaneously for smooth acquisition close, premium multiple durability, and long-dated contract execution.
